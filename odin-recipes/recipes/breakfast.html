Banana Almond Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 300g Non-Fat Greek Yogert
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/4 cup Almond Milk
  • 30g Almonds
  • 1 tablespoon stevia
  • 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized cereal bowl, peel the banana and use a spoon to slice the banana directly into the bowl.
  2. In the same bowl add the 300g of non-fat yogurt.
  3. Add the 1/4 cup of almond milk and 30 g of Almonds.
  4. Slightly mix together the ingredients while being careful not to smash the bananas slices.
  5. Sprinkle the stevia and cinnamon and enjoy!
