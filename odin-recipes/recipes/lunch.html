Taco Salad with Avocado-Lime Dressing

I usually prep the meat, cut the veggies, and make the dressing beforehand and then combine the ingredients together when I want to eat lunch.

The Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage mix (~9 oz)
  • 2 cups chopped romaine
  • 3-4 medium radishes
  • 1/3 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lb ground turkey (lean)
  • 112g tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. In a skillet over medium-high heat, brown the the ground turkey. Once done, use a cullender with a paper towel at the bottom to remove the excess greece. Set aside and allow to cool
  2. Combine the cabbage, romaine, radishes, and cilantro into a large salad bowl.
  3. Once you are ready to eat your salad (I usually prepare ahead of time) add the taco meat to the salad bowl and use tongs to mix evenly.
  4. Add 28g crushed tortilla chips on top and toss in salad dressing.

The Dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon distilled vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 of a large avocado
  • 1/3 cup cilantro (packed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients into a food processor
  2. Turn on food processor and scrape the sides periodically (while the food processor is off) until dressing is creamy.
  3. Pour dressing into an air tight container and store in refrigerator until ready to serve with salad.
